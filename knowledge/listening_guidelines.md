# Listening Guidelines

Use calm, low-energy, acoustic tracks for study, reading, sleep, and decompression.
Use focused, steady-tempo tracks for coding, homework, or deep work.
Use energetic, high-danceability tracks for workouts, parties, cleaning, or motivation.
Use lower-valence tracks only when the user asks for sad, moody, reflective, or nostalgic music.
Avoid recommending extremely intense music for sleep or anxiety relief, even if the genre matches.
When the request is vague, prefer balanced recommendations and explain the uncertainty.
