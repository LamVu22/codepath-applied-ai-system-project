# Genre and Mood Map

lofi: chill, focused, study, coding, quiet, background
ambient: calm, sleep, meditation, low energy
classical: calm, reading, focus, low danceability
pop: happy, upbeat, party, accessible
edm: energetic, dance, workout, high energy
rock: intense, energetic, driving
metal: intense, very high energy
jazz: relaxed, cafe, acoustic, evening
hip hop: focused, confident, rhythmic
synthwave: moody, night drive, electronic
folk: reflective, acoustic, storytelling
country: nostalgic, steady, acoustic
blues: moody, reflective, evening
