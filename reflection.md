# Reflection Notes

High-Energy Pop vs Chill Lofi: The pop profile favors bright, upbeat tracks with higher energy and danceability, while the lofi profile shifts toward lower energy and more acoustic songs, which matches the intended vibe difference.

High-Energy Pop vs Deep Intense Rock: Both prefer high energy, but the rock profile rewards intense mood and faster tempo, so heavier tracks rise above pop songs even when energy is similar.

Chill Lofi vs Deep Intense Rock: These profiles produce almost opposite results because the lofi profile prefers calm, acoustic, lower tempo tracks, while the rock profile pulls toward intense, fast songs with low acousticness.
